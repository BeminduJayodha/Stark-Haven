Our Services

At Stark Haven Logistics, we are committed to providing exceptional service that meets and exceeds your expectations.

  • LTL (Less-Than-Truckload)

    We offer cost-effective LTL shipping with consolidated savings, scheduled departures, and nationwide coverage. Our cross-border expertise ensures smooth and prompt deliveries.

  • FTL (Full Truckload)

    Our FTL services provide faster transit times, reduced damage risk, enhanced security, and increased flexibility. We cater to high-value and time-sensitive deliveries.

  • Temperature Controller Transport

    We specialize in the transport of perishable items, maintaining precise temperature control throughout the journey.

  • Intermodal Shipping

    Combining multiple modes of transportation, our intermodal services offer reduced costs, enhanced efficiency, and improved sustainability. We provide domestic and international intermodal solutions.

  • Expedited Shipping

    For time-sensitive deliveries, we offer same-day, next-day, and expedited air freight options. Our dedicated truckload services ensure speed and control for urgent shipments.

  • Hazmat Transportation

    We handle hazardous materials with the utmost care and compliance, providing safe and secure transport. Our services include over-the-road, intermodal, and temperature-controlled solutions.
